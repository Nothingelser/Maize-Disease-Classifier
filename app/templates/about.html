About CropGuard AI

CropGuard AI helps agronomy teams screen crop leaf images for common disease classes and track outcomes over time.

What the system does

Uploads are processed by a trained classification model and saved as prediction records for analytics, reporting, and follow-up workflows.

Who it is for

This platform is designed for students, researchers, agronomists, and field operators who need quick disease screening support from image evidence.
