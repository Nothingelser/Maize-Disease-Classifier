Disease reference

Quick visual summaries of the disease classes the model is trained to separate.

Northern Leaf Blight

Long grey-green lesions that extend along the leaf veins and enlarge quickly in favorable conditions.

Gray Leaf Spot

Angular brown lesions between veins, often becoming more obvious under humid field conditions.

Healthy

Uniform green tissue with no visible pustules, necrotic streaking, or lesion expansion.

Maize Rust

Reddish-brown pustules scattered across the leaf surface and often concentrated on active lesions.
